### Damage Calculation Reference
| Resource | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Steam Combat Basics Guide** | How damage is calculated (ID: 637093101) |
| **AoE2 Database Damage Formula** | https://www.aoe2database.com/damage_formula/en |
| **UGC Damage Calculation** | https://ugc.aoe2.rocks/general/damage_calculation/ |

**Key Formula:** `(Base Attack - Armor) + (Bonus Damage - Bonus Armor) = Total Damage`
- Minimum damage is 1 (unless bonus damage negates base)
- Each unit has Base Pierce (3), Base Melee (4), and Leitis (31) damage classes
