# Age of Empires 2: Definitive Edition - Civilization Bonuses

Complete documentation of all military and economic bonuses for all 51 civilizations.

## African Civilizations

### Berbers
**Region:** African
**Military Bonuses:**
- Stable units cost -15% in Castle Age, -20% in Imperial Age
- Genitour (unique unit) available
- Camel units +10% faster movement speed

**Economic Bonuses:**
- Villagers move +10% faster
- Stable technologies cost -20%

**Team Bonus:** Genitour available at Archery Range

---

### Ethiopians
**Region:** African
**Military Bonuses:**
- Archers fire 18% faster
- Receive +100 food and +100 gold per age starting in Feudal Age
- Siege weapons fire 18% faster

**Economic Bonuses:**
- Receive +100 food and +100 gold per age starting in Feudal Age

**Team Bonus:** Towers +3 line of sight

---

### Malians
**Region:** African
**Military Bonuses:**
- Barracks units cost -15% in Feudal Age, -20% in Castle Age, -25% in Imperial Age

**Economic Bonuses:**
